      Evidence boundary

      These are architecture demonstrations, not flight validation.

      Shown: authority separation, immediate demotion, unknown-evidence handling, Anchor survival invariants, bounded ground recovery, and explicit negative controls.

      Not shown: operational detector performance, complete causal models, certification, flight readiness, sentinel safety, or proof that a fictional narrative maps directly to a real spacecraft.
